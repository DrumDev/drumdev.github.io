What is DrumDev?

DrumDev is a project created by two friends who love messing about with web technologies.

We are always studying new features and looking for creative and intuitive ways to put them into use!

One of us is a Web Systems Development Graduate currently working as a Web Developer and the other is a Software Development Student working towards a Developer role in the near future.

We are not a business or a company.
